Vibe App

Vibe (Variable Infradian Biological Efficiency) is a FemTech solution designed to bridge the gap between the linear 24-hour society and the natural 28-day infradian rhythm of the female body. Moving beyond basic period tracking, Vibe acts as a "Hormonal GPS" that correlates cycle phases with energy and stress levels. By translating complex biological data into actionable insights, the app empowers users to "surf" their hormonal waves—optimizing productivity, rest, and exercise to reclaim control over their month.

Iphone 15 Clone

This project is a front-end clone of Apple’s iPhone 15 product page. Built with React, styled with Tailwind CSS, and featuring animations powered by GSAP (GreenSock Animation Platform) and Three.js 3D effects. The goal was to replicate the smooth scroll-based animations and polished, responsive design of Apple’s original site.
